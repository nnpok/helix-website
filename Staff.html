PASSION

INDEPENDENCE

A majority of the assigned projects will come with freedom to implement. This requires an ability to work under limited guidance.

AMBITION

Our members are dreamers. We highly value the ability to look forward and set ambitious goals and see them to completion.





Application Process

After submitting the online application, our team will review your submission on a rolling basis. In addition to the student qualities, we will be looking for the qualities we have listed above, and if you are a match, we will contact you individually to schedule an interview. To prepare for the interview, we encourage you to think about why you really want to join our team and the experiences that you will bring to the table. At this point, if pertinent to the position for which you are applying, we would also like to see any previous work. The interview will be mainly behavioral, as we believe that technical skill is something you can always learn, but passion is innate. We look forward to reviewing your applications and look forward to meeting you at interviews!
